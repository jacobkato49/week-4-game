The Ultimate Crystal Collector

Game Directions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your score surpasses the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked. Each new game, will have a new value.

Goodluck!
